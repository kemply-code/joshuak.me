🔧 What I Use

This page is a little look behind the scenes — the hardware and software that helps me build, create, learn, and occasionally procrastinate just a bit more efficiently 😅.

💼 My Gear

  • MacBook Air (M2, 2022) – My daily driver. Quiet, lightweight, and handles everything I throw at it.
  • Yunzii AL66 – Clicky, compact, and feels great to type on. Might be my favourite tool.
  • MX Master 3S – Ergonomic, silent, and full of shortcuts I probably still haven’t discovered.
  • MSI 34" Ultrawide Monitor – Makes multitasking feel like a breeze, and editing is way more fun.
  • BambuLabs A1 Combo – My 3D printer setup. Great for prototyping, printing gifts, or just cool things I find online.
  • IKEA Alex Desk Hack – A custom setup using drawers and a Saljan top — clean, minimal, and all mine.

💻 My Software Stack

  • Arc Browser – A sleek, tab-loving browser that keeps me focused and organized (most of the time).
  • Godot – My go-to engine for experimenting with interactivity and little ideas.
  • Shapr3D – Fast, intuitive CAD for designing models — works great on Mac and iPad.
  • BambuStudio – Slicer that pairs perfectly with my printer. Intuitive and powerful.
  • Apple Software – I rely on Reminders, Calendar, and Notes way more than I’d like to admit. Simple but effective.
  • Raycast – Like Spotlight, but on rocket fuel. Speeds up my workflow in awesome ways.
  • Spotify – Whether it’s coding, walking, or chilling — there’s always a soundtrack playing.
