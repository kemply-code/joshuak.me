all about me

this isn’t just some generic about page. it’s the detailed version — who i am, what i build, and why.

my lore

born in cambridge, england, 2011. early memories include foggy windows, kettle whistles, and the quiet buzz of curiosity.

moved to brisbane at 7 — sun was louder, trees taller, accents different. it changed how i saw the world. for the better.

how i learn

i learn best by doing. repetition, interaction, building. that’s my classroom. my hands teach my brain what lectures can't.

my tech journey

it started with scratch during the pandemic. evolved into robotics, 3D printing, python, arduino, and then c, html, godot, unity.

i built my first FLL robot and never looked back — code and circuits became my playground.

projects that light me up

i’ve built games, AI tools like nameflipper, and small daily fixes like 3D-printed cable hooks.

right now, i’m learning pcb design while building a custom macro pad. it's weirdly thrilling.

high school & beyond

i don’t hate school — i love when it connects to real life. robotics, debating, and deep dives into how things work.

not chasing A’s. chasing understanding.

interests that spiral

i fall into rabbit holes: f1 telemetry, medieval engineering, ethics of AI. if it sparks something in me — i follow it.

current obsessions: sound systems, html, digital presence, blogging.

creativity outside the code

i, build, play, soundtrack my days. sometimes it’s a lo-fi playlist, other times it’s digital painting experiments.

not everything works out — but that’s part of the fun.

philosophy of joshua

build before you’re ready. share before it’s perfect. learn by doing. and trust that small things grow big over time.

why i made this page

i made this to connect — not to impress. to share curiosity, creativity, and the weird joy of making things online.

this site isn’t a portfolio. it’s my corner of the internet where i get to be me.

random facts about me

  • i’ve started more projects than i’ve finished. and that’s okay.
  • i walk to think. a lot.
  • i’ve abseiled into a cave.
